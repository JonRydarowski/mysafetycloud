About My Safety Cloud

We're all about the idea of Family First - however you define it. As with any family there are those items that you want to share and others you want to keep private. By taking a family first approach to managing data, My Safety Cloud has been able to create a simple yet sophisticated approach to family-data management.

Get started today

Secure - First

Priority #1 - Keeping your data safe

My Safety Cloud is serious about data security. We use the highest tier of security, encryption and cipher suites. Meanwhile your data is housed in an undisclosed datacenter protected 24/7 by armed and licensed security personnel.

Learn More


Simple - Second

Priority #2 - A simple user experience

There's beauty in simplicity. Our second priority is to provide you and your family with a smart solution + user experience for managing a robust suite of security + access capabilities that adjust to your family's unique needs.

Learn More


Scalable - Third

Priority #3 - A solution that grows with your family
